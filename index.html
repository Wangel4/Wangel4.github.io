About Me

My name is Willie Angel. I am a student at Madison Park Technical Vocational High School in Boston, Massachusetts enrolled in the Computer Programming and Web Development vocation for the past 4 years. My primary interest is software development; I like creating things with code and solving problems in the process. It can be frustrating but is ultimately very satisfying once things fall into place.

While most people do not even begin coding until after high school, I already have four years of formal training. This puts me at an advantage as I move on to my post-secondary plans.

On this site, you will find examples of coding work completed during my vocational coursework as well as my resume. Please contact me if you have any questions.
