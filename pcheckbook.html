This page displays a checkbook that will track your budget on bills and other stuff you will have to pay for and it will show you what the total of all those expenses are. This program was meant to teach us about financial literacy and learning to be smart with what you spend your money on. The challenge with this program was getting the JavaScript down, since this was one of our first programs working with Javascript. After getting the JavaScript done I had to figure out how to style the page which was easier than the JavaScript, most of the styles use BootStrap 5.
